Comprehensive Health Screenings

Our city offers advanced health screenings to provide a thorough understanding of your health status and potential risks. These screenings include:

  • VO2 Max Testing: Assess your cardiovascular fitness and endurance levels.
  • DEXA Scans: Measure bone density and body composition for a detailed analysis of your physical health.
  • Blood-Based Diagnostics: Comprehensive blood tests to monitor biomarkers related to metabolic health, inflammation, and more.
  • Skin Screenings: Early detection of skin conditions and cancers through advanced imaging techniques.
  • Cancer and Dementia Early Detection: Utilize cutting-edge technology for early detection of various cancers and cognitive decline.
