Comprehensive Health Screens

Welcome to Longevity City, your destination for optimal health and well-being. We are proud to offer a comprehensive range of health screening services inspired by the recommendations of renowned longevity experts Peter Attia and Bryan Johnson. Our data-driven approach emphasizes early detection and prevention of diseases to help you live a longer, healthier life.

Disclaimer: This information is intended for educational purposes and should not replace professional medical advice. Please consult your healthcare provider before undertaking any new health screenings.

1. Comprehensive Blood Panels

At Longevity City, we provide extensive blood testing to assess your overall health:

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC): Detects disorders like anemia and infection.
  • Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP): Evaluates organ function, blood sugar levels, and electrolyte balance.
  • Lipid Profile:
    • Standard Lipid Panel: Measures total cholesterol, LDL, HDL, and triglycerides.
    • Advanced Lipid Testing: Includes ApoB and LDL-P for accurate cardiovascular risk assessment.
  • Inflammatory Markers:
    • C-Reactive Protein (CRP): Identifies inflammation, a risk factor for heart disease.
    • Homocysteine Levels: Elevated levels may indicate cardiovascular risk.
  • Hormone Panels:
    • Thyroid Function Tests: TSH, Free T3, and Free T4 to assess thyroid health.
    • Sex Hormones: Testosterone, estrogen, and progesterone levels.
  • Blood Sugar Regulation:
    • Hemoglobin A1c: Indicates average blood sugar levels over the past 3 months.
    • Fasting Insulin and Glucose: Evaluates insulin sensitivity and diabetes risk.
  • Vitamin and Mineral Levels:
    • Vitamin D, B12, Iron Studies: Detect deficiencies affecting overall health.

2. Genetic Testing

Our genetic screenings help you understand your health risks:

  • Whole Genome Sequencing: Identifies genetic predispositions to various diseases for personalized prevention strategies.
  • Pharmacogenomic Testing: Determines how you may respond to certain medications.
  • Disease-Specific Genetic Markers:
    • BRCA1 and BRCA2 Testing: Assesses risk for breast and ovarian cancer.
    • APOE Genotyping: Evaluates risk for Alzheimer's disease.

3. Cardiovascular Assessments

Protect your heart health with our advanced screenings:

  • Coronary Artery Calcium (CAC) Scan: Detects calcified plaque in coronary arteries.
  • Carotid Intima-Media Thickness (CIMT) Test: Measures carotid artery wall thickness to assess stroke risk.
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG) and Echocardiogram: Evaluates heart rhythm and structure.
  • Endothelial Function Test: Assesses blood vessel lining health.
  • High-Sensitivity Troponin Test: Detects minor heart damage.

4. Cancer Screenings

Early detection is key; we offer:

  • Colonoscopy or CT Colonography: Recommended starting at age 45 to detect colon cancer.
  • Mammography and Breast MRI: Annual screenings for women starting at age 40.
  • Prostate-Specific Antigen (PSA) Test: For men over 50 to screen for prostate cancer.
  • Skin Cancer Screening:
    • Annual Dermatological Exams: Regular skin checks by our dermatologists.
    • Mole Mapping and Dermatoscopic Imaging: Monitors changes in moles and skin lesions.
    • Self-Examination Guidance: Learn how to perform regular self-checks.
  • Liquid Biopsy Tests: Cutting-edge blood tests that detect circulating tumor DNA.
  • Cancer Biomarker Tests:
    • CA-125 Test: For ovarian cancer screening.
    • Alpha-Fetoprotein (AFP): For liver cancer screening.

5. Imaging Studies

Our state-of-the-art imaging services include:

  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI):
    • Brain MRI: Detects early signs of neurodegenerative diseases.
    • Full-Body MRI: Screens for tumors and abnormalities without radiation exposure.
  • Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) Scan:
    • Bone Density Measurement: Assesses osteoporosis risk.
    • Body Composition Analysis: Evaluates fat mass, lean mass, and bone mineral content.
  • Coronary CT Angiography: Provides detailed images of heart arteries.
  • Panoramic Dental X-rays: Identifies dental and jawbone abnormalities.

6. Metabolic and Fitness Assessments

Optimize your physical health with our assessments:

  • VO2 Max Testing: Determines cardiovascular fitness and aerobic endurance.
  • Resting Metabolic Rate (RMR) Test: Measures calories burned at rest.
  • Body Composition Analysis:
    • Bioelectrical Impedance Analysis (BIA): Assesses fat mass, muscle mass, and body water.
    • Electrical Impedance Myography (EIM): Evaluates muscle health.

7. Diabetes and Blood Sugar Monitoring

Manage and monitor your glucose levels:

  • Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM): Real-time tracking to optimize diet and detect insulin resistance.
  • Oral Glucose Tolerance Test (OGTT): Assesses sugar processing over time.

8. Microbiome and Digestive Health

Discover the balance of your gut flora:

  • Gut Microbiome Testing: Evaluates bacteria affecting digestion and immunity.
  • Comprehensive Stool Analysis: Assesses digestive function and potential pathogens.
  • Organic Acids Test (OAT): Evaluates metabolic function and nutrient deficiencies.

9. Cognitive and Mental Health Evaluations

Prioritize your mental well-being:

  • Neurocognitive Testing:
    • Comprehensive Cognitive Assessments: Detect early signs of dementia and cognitive impairments.
    • Computerized Cognitive Testing: Quick screenings for cognitive decline.
  • Genetic Testing for Dementia Risk:
    • APOE Genotyping: Identifies risk for Alzheimer's disease.
  • Brain Imaging Studies:
    • Functional MRI (fMRI): Assesses brain activity.
    • PET Scans: Detects amyloid plaques associated with Alzheimer's.
  • Biomarker Testing:
    • Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) Analysis: Measures proteins linked to Alzheimer's.
  • Mental Health Screenings: Evaluates for depression, anxiety, and other conditions.
  • Stress and Resilience Questionnaires: Measures stress levels and coping mechanisms.

10. Sleep Studies

Improve your sleep quality:

  • Polysomnography: Diagnoses sleep disorders like sleep apnea.
  • Home Sleep Tests: Convenient options for initial screening.
