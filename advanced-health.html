Comprehensive Health Screenings

Our city offers a range of advanced health screenings designed to provide a thorough understanding of your health status and potential risks. These screenings include:

  • VO2 Max Testing: Assess your cardiovascular fitness and endurance levels.
  • DEXA Scans: Measure bone density and body composition for a detailed analysis of your physical health.
  • Blood-Based Diagnostics: Comprehensive blood tests to monitor biomarkers related to metabolic health, inflammation, and more.
  • Skin Screenings: Early detection of skin conditions and cancers through advanced imaging techniques.
  • Cancer and Dementia Early Detection: Utilize cutting-edge technology for early detection of various cancers and cognitive decline.

Inspired by Leading Health Experts

Our health programs draw inspiration from renowned experts like Peter Attia and Bryan Johnson, focusing on longevity and optimal health. We incorporate their insights into our wellness strategies, ensuring residents have access to the latest advancements in health science.

Personalized Health Plans

Each resident receives a personalized health plan tailored to their unique needs and goals. Our plans include:

  • Mental Health Support: Access to mental health resources and support networks.
